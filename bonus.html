Harlequin Gorilla

Harlequin Gorilla, the superhero, is a powerful and agile champion of justice, known for his unwavering commitment to protecting the planet and its inhabitants. With super strength, enhanced speed and agility, and a keen sense of environmental responsibility, Green Gorilla is always ready to leap into action to stop those who threaten the natural world. Despite his tough exterior, Green Gorilla has a heart of gold and a deep love for all living things, and will stop at nothing to ensure a safe and healthy planet for future generations.
