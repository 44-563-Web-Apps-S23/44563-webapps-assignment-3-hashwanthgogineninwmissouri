Basketball

Basketball is, in my opinion, the best activity I can do with my friends to have fun and burn off some energy. Instead of spending the entire day on the couch playing video games or watching television, I believe that playing basketball is a pleasant and simple way to pass the time. Basketball is one of my favorite sports because you can play it alone or with teammates and you don't need any equipment. I prefer playing with others to playing alone.
